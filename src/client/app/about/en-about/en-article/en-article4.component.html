The first generation of skin-listening devices was born

The first generation of skin listening devices is based on the invention of the patented "transformer skin listening device". The hearing effects of the first generation of skin listening devices can be summarized as follows

  • 1.Almost all sounds are very sensitive, such as voice, door, phone, walking, cough and ambient sound;
  • 2.After training, deaf people can tell a few simple sounds;
  • 3.Ability to distinguish between voice, door and walk;
  • 4.For the beat of music, there is a distinct sense of perception and analysis, which may be the most primitive sound effect.

The first generation of skin sound organ is constructed and principle :

  • 1.The microphone converts an external sound signal to a current pulse signal ;
  • 2.Audio amplification amplifies the audio signal ;
  • 3.The audio booster overcomes the resistance of the human skin, providing high-voltage communication signals for the drive plane electrode ;
  • 4.The battery supplies the audio amplifier ;
  • 5.The flat electrode is used to stimulate the skin and pass the pulse current through the skin to the brain, as shown in figure 1.

In figure 1, you can see that the speaker is not used in the skin listening device, so there is no relationship to the original auditory system in the process. In fact, the first generation of skin listening devices was a modified version of a common hearing aid that changed the hearing aid's headphones into a booster, the pacifier. The disadvantage is that the ability to distinguish speech is extremely limited, but can be used as an acoustic experimental device.

In the previous experiment, we could see that the human skin's response to an impulse current was at least 0.1 Hz ~ 30kHz. The threshold of the voltage that gives the skin a sensation is approximately 1 ~ 6V, and the general signal voltage should exceed 2 ~ 12V to ensure the feeling of the skin. But it's hard to generate such a high voltage for a typical portable electronic device.
