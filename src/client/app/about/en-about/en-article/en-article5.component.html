High-performance skin listening device

The second generation of skin listening devices mainly USES multichannel bandpass filter and array electrode technology. This technique involves content includes: determine the voice frequency range, design and production of band-pass filter circuit, and determine the channel number, determine the center frequency of each channel, making electrode array, arrangement and production is determined, connected to the microphone and microphone amplifier circuit, multi-channel power amplification circuit, multi-channel step-up transformer, design and production of high efficiency and small volume of the step-up transformer.
