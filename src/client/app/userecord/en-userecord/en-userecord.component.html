On March 13, 2006, we conducted a small batch trial summary, and found that many of the practical problems are difficult to popularize the skin listening device:

  • The students were not able to find the point causing the signal to be uncomfortable and unstable
  • Because of deaf children for a long time no voice signal, are used to sign language, so there is no voice, the concept of no for the urgent request of sound signal
  • The experiment equipment is not perfect, only simple tape recorder, no such as VCD, TV, electronic instrument and other supporting equipment
  • Traditional sign language teaching and a lot of social engagement have been used throughout the course of the process, with little time for the trial and training of the skin listening device
  • Unable to communicate with normal people, deaf students lack a lot of common sense, they will stick the back of the electrode on the skin, and they will not be able to replace the electrode in time
  • During the use of the process, the deaf student continuously adjusts the switch by hand and affects the normal work
  • Because of the lack of knowledge, some parents have doubts about the safety of their skin
